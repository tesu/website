Weeb Stuff

I don't watch anime anymore. I stopped watching seasonal anime after 2017, and completely stopped after 2018.

My favorite anime studio is (was?) Studio Trigger. I'm a huge fan of Kill la Kill, Inferno Cop, and Space Patrol Luluco. I also really enjoy Type-Moon stuff. No love for Fate/Grand Order though, and the localization for Mahoutsukai no Yoru came out way too late for me to care.

I had a brief stint with fansubbing in 2017. I was an editor for Chihiro-Fansubs, but quit after two seasons due to lack of motivation. Fansubbing is dead.

I've attended many anime conventions (Otakon, Katsucon, Anime Boston, Anime Central, Anime NYC) but they're whatever to me. I don't care about cosplay, all the fan panels suck, and I usually end up purchasing merch that I could get cheaper online. I do enjoy their arcade sections (usually rented from Tokyo Attack); my go-to games include DDR/ITG, Sound Voltex, and jubeat.
